\# Privacy Policy for CurrencySwap - Currency Converter



CurrencySwap does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.



The extension works locally in your browser. It scans visible webpage text to detect supported currency values and convert prices into your selected target currency.



\## Data Collection



CurrencySwap does not collect:



\- Personal information

\- Browsing history

\- Login information

\- Payment information

\- Cookies

\- Analytics data

\- Location data



\## How the Extension Works



CurrencySwap reads visible webpage text in order to detect prices and currency codes. This processing happens locally in your browser.



The extension may request exchange rate data from an external currency exchange rate service in order to calculate conversions. No personal data is sent with these requests.



\## Data Sharing



CurrencySwap does not sell, share, or transmit personal data to third parties.



\## Permissions



CurrencySwap requires access to webpages so it can detect and convert visible prices. These permissions are used only for currency conversion functionality.
